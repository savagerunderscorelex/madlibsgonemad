Mad Libs Gone Mad

What do you do on Mad Libs Gone Mad?

You're prompted to enter some adverbs, nouns, verbs, pronouns, and adjectives, and then a story including those words you've added will appear in your screen. Like a regular Mad Lib!

What is a Mad Lib?

Mad Libs is a word game that includes being prompted for multiple words, that is then inserted in a story with blanks. Wikipedia
